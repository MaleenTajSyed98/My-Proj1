Our Vision

Towards Excellence in Promoting Need based Training Programs and Research in Learning Technologies and E-Content Development.

Our Mission

To design and develop need-based training programs for Polytechnic & Engineering faculty and professionals.

To offer international training through ITEC/SCAAP for global educators and professionals.

To develop innovative programs in collaboration with Universities and Institutions.

To support Government of India schemes in technical education.

To provide research, testing, and consultancy services for industry and government.

To partner with industries for skill-based and industrial training.

To design instructional systems and multimedia content using modern technologies.
